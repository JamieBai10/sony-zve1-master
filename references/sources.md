## Dynamic-content rule

Always live-check Sony official sources for firmware availability/version, downloads, software/service behavior, compatibility lists, product alerts, warranty/support terms, licensing, regional models, and current prices/availability. A local manual is not sufficient evidence for these.

## Scope boundaries

This skill covers the **ZV-E1 body**. For an attached lens, flash, microphone, wireless remote, mount adapter, computer, smartphone, or third-party accessory, combine the ZV-E1 guide with the exact accessory's official compatibility/manual source. Do not infer compatibility from a mechanically matching mount or connector.
